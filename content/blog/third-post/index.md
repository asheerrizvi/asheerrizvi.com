A couple of days ago, I decided on a whim to write a blog. I wasn't clear what purpose would it serve. I do not consider myself an accomplished developer yet and so what could I offer? At the same time one should be able to gauge their progress and learn from what they did in the past.

I then decided to use this blog as a time capsule. A personal ledger of my code which I can refer to and see where I was. Five or ten years down the line I would like to sit back and read this blog with a coffee in my hand and a smile on my face.

For others who may stumble upon this remote corner of the web. I am glad you are here, please have a look, give suggestions or critique my work. I would love to get suggestions from one and all.
